Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 9, 2026

coAI ("coAI," "we," "us," or "our") provides a private, mobile-first transaction chat and document support platform for active real estate transactions. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect information when you use our website, product, SMS invites, authentication features, and related services (collectively, the "Service").

1. Scope

This Privacy Policy applies to people who visit our website, create an account, are invited into a transaction by a real estate professional, or otherwise interact with the Service.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect the following categories of information:

  • Name, phone number, email address, and role in a transaction
  • Authentication and account data, including login events, one-time passcode verification, invite status, and session details
  • Transaction information, such as property address, deadlines, contacts, and related deal details
  • Uploaded files, documents, messages, and chat history
  • Device, browser, IP address, and basic usage or security log data
  • Information provided to us by the Realtor or transaction professional who invited you to the Service

3. How We Use Information

We may use information to:

  • Provide, operate, secure, and maintain the Service
  • Create and manage transaction access for invited participants
  • Send transactional SMS and email communications, including invite links and one-time passcodes
  • Verify identity and help prevent unauthorized access
  • Process documents and generate transaction-related AI assistance
  • Maintain audit, security, fraud prevention, and troubleshooting records
  • Comply with legal obligations and enforce our terms

4. AI and Automated Processing

coAI may use automated systems and third-party AI providers to analyze uploaded documents, extract structured information, answer transaction questions, and support safety or escalation workflows. AI-generated output may be incomplete or inaccurate and should not be treated as legal, tax, financial, or wire instruction advice.

5. SMS Communications

coAI uses SMS for transactional communications related to a specific real estate transaction, such as secure invite links, one-time passcodes, and limited account or access notices. Message frequency varies. Message and data rates may apply.

Reply STOP to opt out of SMS messages. Reply HELP for help. We do not use SMS under this MVP for marketing or promotional messages.

6. How We Share Information

We may share information:

  • With the real estate professional or transaction participants connected to the relevant transaction
  • With service providers that help us operate the Service, such as cloud hosting, authentication, storage, messaging, email, analytics, security, and AI processing providers
  • If required by law, legal process, or to protect rights, safety, or the integrity of the Service
  • In connection with a merger, acquisition, financing, or sale of all or part of our business

We do not sell or rent personal information, mobile phone numbers, or SMS opt-in data to third parties for their own marketing or promotional purposes. Text messaging opt-in data and consent are not shared with third parties except vendors or service providers that support delivery of the Service.

7. Data Retention

We retain information for as long as reasonably necessary to provide the Service, maintain security and audit records, comply with law, resolve disputes, and enforce agreements. Retention periods may vary based on the type of data and the nature of the transaction.

8. Your Choices and Requests

You may contact us to request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal information, subject to legal, security, and operational limitations. If you are an invited transaction participant, we may coordinate certain requests with the Realtor or transaction professional associated with the transaction.

You may also opt out of SMS messages by replying STOP, or contact us at support@coai.com.

9. Security

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational safeguards designed to protect information. However, no method of transmission or storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

10. Children's Privacy

The Service is not directed to children under 18, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make material changes, we will update the effective date above.

12. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at support@coai.com.
